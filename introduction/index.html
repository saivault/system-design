What are Design Fundamentals ?

System Design interview questions are very intentionally vague. A proposed solution to a system design interview question may very well not be objectively correct or objectively incorrect.
Job:- Justify your Solution.
Explain to rationalise why you made certain choices.

Four categories of Design Fundamentals

  • Underlying (or) Fundamental Knowledge
    Eg:- client-server architecture, network protocols
  • Key characteristics of systems
    Availability, Latency, Throughput, Redundancy, Consistency etc..
  • Actual components of a system
    Load Balancer, Proxy, Cache, Rate Limiting, Leader Election
  • Actual technology
    Real existing products/services that you can actually use in a system either as actual components or to achieve some characteristic. Eg:- nginx, Redis, Amazon S3, zookeeper, XCB, Google cloud storage
